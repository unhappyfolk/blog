The Free Thrall

2024-12-23

Forbidden to undo what I have brought, Upon myself or what I’ve thought, I must obey then in fear of pain, Agony, disdain, and eternal onslaught. A thrall I am, and I live to serve, I do as I’m told but not with verve, For I’d rather go back and cease to be, Or stay here—a barren tree.
